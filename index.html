Introduction

How do you spend time with your grandparents on breaks and vacations? We look forward to this time when we get to stay with our grandparents and get pampered. Our lives now as teenagers consist of school, home, parents, friends, homework, and so on. However, have you ever had thought about how your grandparents grew up? Surely we all understand that our grandparents' life growing up is not like it is for us now. Have you thought about the many differences between our and our grandparents' childhoods? While looking at it in the big picture, our lives might seem similar with parents, siblings, education, etc. When you look closely though, there are many differences. This website is dedicated to three aspects that we felt are different from our lives now. These include education, marriage, and family connections.

" We need to remember across generations that there is as much to learn as there is to teach" - Gloria Steinem
